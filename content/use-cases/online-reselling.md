# Price Research for Online Resellers

CostCam helps eBay, Poshmark, Mercari and Facebook Marketplace sellers price listings in seconds.

## Problem
- Listings sit unsold when over-priced
- Money left on the table when under-priced
- Every item needs fresh research
- Comp-hunting on eBay sold listings is slow

## How It Works
Snap a photo to get:
- Item identification with brand and model
- Estimated price range and typical sale price
- Condition notes that affect value
- Suggested search keywords for your listing title
- Direct links to comparable listings on eBay, Amazon and Google Shopping

## Success Stories

### Sneaker Flip
- Found: Air Jordans at thrift store ($25)
- Value: $180-220 used
- Action: Listed on eBay at $199
- Result: Sold within 48 hours

### Vintage Lamp
- Found: Mid-century lamp at estate sale ($15)
- Value: $90-140
- Action: Listed on Facebook Marketplace at $120
- Result: Sold to local buyer, no shipping hassle

## Listing Tips
1. Use the brand and model in your title for search visibility
2. Price slightly under the typical estimate to move inventory faster
3. Mention condition keywords from the analysis
4. Cross-post to multiple platforms with the same description
5. Use the search-keyword suggestions to find similar sold listings
